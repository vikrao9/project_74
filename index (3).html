FPS
Won in 2013

FPS is a non-profit educational program that organizes academic competitions in which students apply critical thinking and problem-solving skills to hypothetical future situations. The program looks at current technological, geopolitical, and societal trends and projects those trends 20-30 years into the future in order to train students to develop solutions to the challenges they may face as adults
